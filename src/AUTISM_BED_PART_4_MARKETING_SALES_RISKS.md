## 3. RISKS & MITIGATION

### Critical Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|-----------|
| **Child injury during use** | Low | Catastrophic | Exceed all safety standards (CPSIA, ASTM, IEC); comprehensive $5M+ insurance |
| **Manufacturing delays** | Medium | High | Dual-source suppliers, 4-week production buffer, penalty clauses |
| **Competitor market entry** | Medium | High | Build brand moat with patents, clinical evidence, community; move fast |
| **Low customer adoption** | Medium | High | Aggressive beta program, referral incentives, flexible pricing |
| **Cash flow shortage** | Medium | High | Raise 12-month funding runway, focus on high-margin sales first |
| **Slow B2B sales cycle** | Medium | High | Expect 9–12 month facility cycle, maintain strong DTC to bridge |
| **Privacy/data breach** | Low | Catastrophic | HIPAA/GDPR-compliant AES-256 encryption, annual security audits, cyber liability insurance |

### Manufacturing Risks

- **Component shortage:** Dual-source critical components, maintain 6-week safety stock
- **Quality defects:** 100% pre-shipment testing, supplier audits, ISO certifications
- **Raw material inflation:** Lock in 12-month fixed-price contracts, efficiency improvements
- **Logistics disruption:** Multiple logistics partners, air freight option, domestic fallback

### Market Risks

- **Competitive pressure:** Build patent protection, publish clinical evidence, community moat
- **DTC channel saturation:** Diversify early (B2B, insurance, partnerships), focus on organic/SEO
- **Insurance pathway fails:** Don't over-rely; focus on DTC + B2B first

### Compliance Risks

- **FDA re-classification as medical device:** Proactive FDA engagement, conservative marketing language
- **State toy safety law changes:** Exceed federal CPSIA standards, legal review for each state
- **ADA accessibility tightening:** Design with accessibility from start, regular compliance audits
