## 2. PRODUCT MODALITIES & PRICING

### Model 1: Basic Sensory Bed

**Features:**
- Titanium-aluminum frame (adjustable height 15"–22")
- Full-size mattress surface (54" × 76")
- Breathable mesh enclosure (top half)
- Non-contact pressure sensors (3 zones)
- PEMF, vibroacoustic, chromotherapy, aromatherapy (3-chamber)
- Wearable HRV sensor (Bluetooth)
- Mobile app + web dashboard
- Emergency e-stop button
- Hypoallergenic foam padding

**Manufacturing Cost:** $920 COGS  
**Retail Price:** $2,995  
**Wholesale (B2B):** $1,800–$2,200 (40% discount)  
**Gross Margin:** 68%
